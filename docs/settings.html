Settings

The settings can be accessed from the settings tab inside the Sidebar.

Resolution

Sets the rendering resolution of the scene. By default it’s adjusted to fix the browser window, but a fixed one can also be specified. This is especially useful for creating screenshots and recordings.

The scene gets scaled accordingly to fit the browser if a fixed resolution is provided. While the aspect ratio is preserved, it might look zoomed in or out.

Warning

Recordings with high resolution might lead to frame drops depending on your hardware.

Recordings

Here you can only specify the framerate of the recordings.

Stage

Loads a stage into the scene or removes the current. Also shows the download progress if one was provided via url parameters.
